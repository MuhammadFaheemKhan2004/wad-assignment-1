Academics Overview

The department offers PhD, MPhil, MS (Information Science & Technology), M.Sc. and BS degrees. Each degree programme has a specific objective and focus. The course of study and syllabi for each degree are updated and in line with its objectives.

Degree Programs

PhD Program

PhD is the highest degree in an academic discipline and provides an opportunity for students to perform original research. More Details

MPhil Program

The MPhil program trains students to conduct research and is typically completed in two years (4 semesters). More Details

MS Program

The MS in Information Science & Technology program is designed to prepare students for careers in information management and technology development. More Details

MS Data Science

The MS Data Science program equips students with skills in data analysis, visualization, and big data analytics. More Details

BS Program

The BS Computer Science program combines theoretical and practical knowledge in computer science and software development. More Details
